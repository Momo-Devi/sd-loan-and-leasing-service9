Commercial Hire Purchase: Flexible Business Financing Solutions

What is a Commercial Hire Purchase?

A Commercial Hire Purchase (CHP) is a business finance option that allows companies to acquire vehicles or equipment by making fixed monthly payments over a set term. Ownership is transferred to the business once the final payment is made.

Key Benefits of Commercial Hire Purchase

  • Fixed Payments: Predictable monthly costs for easier budgeting.
  • Immediate Equipment Use: Use the vehicle or equipment while making payments.
  • Flexible Loan Terms: Choose repayment periods that suit your business cash flow.
  • Tax Benefits: Potential tax deductions on interest and depreciation.
  • Ownership at End of Term: Gain full ownership after the final payment.

How Does a Commercial Hire Purchase Work?

The process is simple and beneficial for businesses:

  1. Select the vehicle or equipment your business needs.
  2. Agree on the hire purchase term and repayment plan.
  3. Make fixed monthly payments while using the asset.
  4. Once all payments are completed, ownership is transferred to your business.
